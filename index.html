📖 Prayer Info
Fajr — 2 rak'ah Sunnah + 2 rak'ah Fard. Must be prayed before sunrise.
Dhuhr — 4 rak'ah Sunnah + 4 rak'ah Fard + 2 rak'ah Sunnah. Midday prayer.
Asr — 4 rak'ah Fard. Afternoon prayer. Must be prayed before sunset.
Maghrib — 3 rak'ah Fard + 2 rak'ah Sunnah. Immediately after sunset.
Isha — 4 rak'ah Fard + 2 rak'ah Sunnah + 3 Witr. Night prayer.
